SMVITM

Where Values And Technology Converge

Founded in 2010 by H. H. Shri Vishwavallabha Theertha Swamiji of Shri Sode Vadiraja Mutt – one of the 700 plus years old Ashta Mutts associated with the world famous Shri Krishna temple of Udupi in Karnataka – Shri MadhwaVadiraja Institute of Technology & Management (SMVITM), situated at Bantakal in Udupi has carved a niche for itself in imparting quality engineering education in the coastal Karnataka region. I, Niveditha, a final year Computer Science student have created my college website to show a glimpse of the well established institution.

About

At present, SMVITM, with a total student intake of 441 per year, hopefully more and more in the upcoming years, offers Bachelor of Engineering courses in AI and ML, AI and Data Science, Computer Science, Civil, Mechanical, Electronicc and Engineering. Some of them are mentioned below! Currently, 1562 students are pursuing their engineering education at the institute. Despite being located in a rural area, SMVITM ensures that its students are well trained in existing engineering practices and acquainted with the latest industrial trends.

Department Details

  • CSE:Today, most of the real world activities and fields, right from calculations to space exploration are being computerized as the operations can be much faster and error free with the computer applications. This has resulted in an increased demand for skilled and competent professionals in the computer engineering sector. To cater to this, the Department of Computer Science was established.The Department aims at training the students to be industry-ready by the end of their course through regular curriculum as prescribed by the University and also with supplementary programs, throughout their study.

  • E&C:Electronics today stands at the forefront of the rapidly expanding horizon of Science and Technology. The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering in SMVITM was established in the year 2010.The department is intent on creative and technologically advanced skill transfer to the students through teaching, mentoring and counselling. It regularly organizes seminars, symposiums, workshops and invited talks by eminent faculty from reputed institutions and industry experts.
